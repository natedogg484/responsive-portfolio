About Me

I've always wanted to have a job in which I was creating or building something. I took a Computer Science course in college in which we worked in Windows Basic and I really enjoyed coding. It really made me feel as if I was able to make something. In the long term, working for a gaming company would be my ideal job, but anything that will allow me to learn, grow, and develop will make me happy.

On the personal side I enjoy gaming, snowboarding/skiing, exercising, competing in Spartan Races, and spending time at home with my lady and our cats. I really want to go snowboarding out in the Western United States, Europe, and some other places throughout the world and definitely plan on doing that in the future. I hope to master coding so that I can someday play a game that I created.

I am an animal lover at heart, and ever since I was young cats have been my favorite. I think I enjoy their reclusiveness because it takes work to earn and keep their trust but once you have it they are great and loving animals. Plus, they don't bark.
